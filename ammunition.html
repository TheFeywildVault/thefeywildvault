About Magic Ammunition

With the addition of magic ammunition, such as arrows and bolts, a common question might be "Can magical arrows also recieve the magic benefit from a magic Bow or Quiver?"
✓ Yes, magic projectiles fired from a bow with an additional magic effect will get the benefits of both.
✗ However, magic projectiles drawn from a quiver with an additional effect will not recieve ebenfits from both. All magical Quiver effects specify that only nonmagic arrows may recieve it's bonus effect when the projectile is drawn.
